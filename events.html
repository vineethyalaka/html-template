Ms. Blind Diva 2016

In the words of Beyonce “Diva is a female version of a hustler.” At Eyes Like Mine Inc. a Blind Diva empowers other vision impaired and blind people to tap into their strengths, realize areas in their lives that need improvement, and most of all she is doing it in style. Ms. Blind Diva 2016 works, volunteers, or attends school while making her community aware that blind people don’t just exist; they live! Simultaneously, Ms. Blind Diva is creative in style, talented, and not intimidated to take a chance inspite of her vision loss, and here she is our 2015 Ms. Blind Diva Ms. Trish Ebel
